Members of the UMass Dartmouth Big Data Club love working on
hands-on, technical projects and challenges. Here are some examples
of our previous projects that we have completed, as well as ongoing
coding challenges for new members.

Brown Datathon 2020 Office Challenge

ASA Datafest 2019

Every year, Big Data Club competes at Datafest, a data analysis competition sponsored by the American Statistical Association. At the last competition, we won first place in the Data Visualization category!

Brown Datathon 2020

At the Brown Datathon, we took home second place in the Citizens Bank Homebuyer Challenge by using a graph neural net to predict the number of home purchases in a given area.

Office Challenge

Using a dataset of sensor readings that measure atmospheric variables in a typical office space, can you train a machine learning algorithm to predict whether or not a person is in the room? This is a challenge typically completed by new members of the club.
