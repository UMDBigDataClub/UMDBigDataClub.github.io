Meet the officers of Big Data Club

Salvador Balkus

President

Salvador is a senior Data Science major who has been a Big Data Club member since Fall 2018. He has attended Brown Datathon and the ASA Datafest at Bryant University, and has worked on a variety of data science and computing projects in the club as well. He currently works as a research assistant in the Computational Statistics and Data Science Lab under Professor Julia Fang. Previously, he worked as a research assistant at the UMass Dartmouth Public Policy Center.
